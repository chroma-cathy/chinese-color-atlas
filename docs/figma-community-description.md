# Chinese Color Atlas Sample - Figma Community Listing

## Suggested Title

Chinese Color Atlas Sample - Traditional Chinese Color Variables

## Short Description

A free starter Figma Variables sample with 24 representative Chinese traditional colors, semantic examples, and three palette modes: Guochao, Song-inspired, and Dunhuang.

## Full Description

Chinese Color Atlas curates 567 Chinese traditional colors for designers, developers, and AI creators. This free Figma Community sample gives you a practical starter set for interface exploration without exposing the complete paid atlas.

Inside the sample:

- 24 representative Chinese traditional color primitives
- Three design directions: Guochao, Song-inspired, and Dunhuang
- Semantic examples for surfaces, text, brand accents, and warm status colors
- Color names in English transliteration and Chinese characters
- Attribution and links back to the full project

Use it for:

- Chinese-inspired brand moodboards
- UI color exploration
- Editorial and cultural landing pages
- Design-token experiments before adopting the complete 567-color resource pack
